Mission spotlight

Apollo 11 — the first footprint

One mission, chosen from five, that changed how humanity measured distance.

JULY 1969 · NASA

A quarter million miles, then a single step

Apollo 11 carried Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin, and Michael Collins to the Moon, where Armstrong and Aldrin became the first humans to walk on another world. The landing fulfilled a goal NASA had chased for the entire decade, and it remains the benchmark every later mission is measured against.
